As of June 2026, the Indian Navy and Coast Guard have significantly expanded their fleet under the "Aatmanirbhar Bharat" initiative. The navy has transitioned into a "Builder's Navy," with a record number of indigenous ships commissioned in the last 18 months, including a major triple-commissioning event on June 21, 2026.

Below is the category-wise listing of major Indian naval assets.

### 1. Aircraft Carriers
The Indian Navy currently operates two full-sized aircraft carriers, providing the ability to maintain a presence on both the eastern and western seaboards.
*   **INS Vikrant (R11):** India’s first indigenous aircraft carrier, commissioned in 2022. It is the flagship of the Eastern Fleet.
*   **INS Vikramaditya (R33):** A modified Kiev-class carrier (formerly *Admiral Gorshkov*), serving as the flagship of the Western Fleet.

### 2. Destroyers (Guided Missile)
Destroyers are the "workhorses" of the fleet, providing offensive strike and air defense.
*   **Visakhapatnam-class (Project 15B):** 4 Ships (INS *Visakhapatnam*, *Mormugao*, *Imphal*, and *Surat*). All four were commissioned by early 2025.
*   **Kolkata-class (Project 15A):** 3 Ships (INS *Kolkata*, *Kochi*, *Chennai*).
*   **Delhi-class:** 3 Ships (INS *Delhi*, *Mysore*, *Mumbai*).
*   **Rajput-class:** Remaining active units include INS *Rana*, *Ranvir*, and *Ranvijay*.

### 3. Frigates
The frigate fleet has seen recent growth with the induction of advanced stealth platforms.
*   **Nilgiri-class (Project 17A):** These are the most advanced stealth frigates.
    *   **Active:** INS *Nilgiri*, *Himgiri*, *Udaygiri*, and **INS Dunagiri** (commissioned June 21, 2026).
*   **Shivalik-class (Project 17):** 3 Ships (INS *Shivalik*, *Satpura*, *Sahyadri*).
*   **Talwar-class (Project 1135.6):** 6 Ships in service, plus new **Tushil-class** units recently added from the follow-on order.
*   **Brahmaputra-class:** INS *Brahmaputra*, *Betwa*, and *Beas*.

### 4. Corvettes & ASW Shallow Water Craft
*   **Kamorta-class (Project 28):** 4 indigenous ASW stealth corvettes (INS *Kamorta*, *Kadmatt*, *Kiltan*, *Kavaratti*).
*   **Arnala-class (ASW SWC):** Designed for coastal anti-submarine warfare. **INS Agray** was the latest to join the fleet (June 2026).
*   **Kora-class:** 4 missile corvettes (INS *Kora*, *Kirpan*, *Kulish*, *Karmuk*).
*   **Khukri-class:** Remaining active units like INS *Khanjar* and *Khirpan*.
*   **Veer-class:** High-speed missile boats (several units active).

### 5. Submarines
India maintains a "triad" capability with both nuclear and conventional submarines.
*   **Nuclear-Powered (SSBN):** Arihant-class (INS *Arihant* and INS *Arighat* active; others in trials).
*   **Conventional (SSK):**
    *   **Kalvari-class (Scorpene):** 6 Ships (INS *Kalvari*, *Khanderi*, *Karanj*, *Vela*, *Vagir*, and *Vagsheer*).
    *   **Sindhughosh-class:** Kilo-class submarines (multiple active).
    *   **Shishumar-class:** Type 209 submarines (multiple active).

### 6. Indian Coast Guard (ICG) Ships
The Coast Guard currently operates a fleet of over 150 vessels.
*   **Pollution Control Vessels (PCV):** Samudra-class (3 ships) and the new **GSL-class** (first unit commissioned 2026).
*   **Offshore Patrol Vessels (OPV):**
    *   **Vikram-class:** 7 Ships (e.g., ICGS *Vikram*, *Vijaya*).
    *   **Sachet-class:** 5 Ships (e.g., ICGS *Sachet*, *Sujeet*).
    *   **Samarth-class:** 11 Ships (e.g., ICGS *Samarth*, *Shoor*).
*   **Fast Patrol Vessels (FPV):** **Adamya-class** (latest induction: **ICGS Achal** in May 2026), *Aadesh*-class (20 ships), and *Rajshree*-class (13 ships).
*   **Interceptor Boats:** Over 70 high-speed boats (L&T and ABG classes) for coastal security.

### 7. MPVs (Multi-Purpose Vessels/Vehicles)
The term "MPV" in the Indian military context refers to two distinct assets:
*   **Naval Multi-Purpose Vessels (Ships):** The **Samarthak-class** (built by L&T). These 3,750-ton ships are used for towing, maritime surveillance, and as trial platforms for new weapons.
    *   **INS Samarthak** (First-of-class)
    *   **INS Utkarsh** (Second-of-class)
*   **Mine Protected Vehicles (Land):** The **Aditya MPV** is an armored personnel carrier used by the Indian Navy’s Marine Commandos (MARCOS) and the Coast Guard for land-based coastal security and counter-insurgency operations.

### 8. Other Critical Assets
*   **Amphibious Warfare:** INS *Jalashwa* (LPD) and several *Shardul*-class and *Magar*-class landing ships.
*   **Survey Vessels:** The new **Sandhayak-class (Large)**, including the recently commissioned **INS Sanshodhak** (June 21, 2026).
*   **Replenishment Ships:** INS *Deepak*, *Shakti*, and *Jyoti*.
